info Vacancy Details & Pay Scale

The Staff Selection Commission will hold the Combined Graduate Level Examination, 2024 for filling up of various Group ‘B’ and Group ‘C’ posts in different Ministries/ Departments/ Organizations of Government of India.

Group B Gazetted

Pay Level-8 (₹47,600 to ₹1,51,100)

Group B Non-Gazetted

Pay Level-7 (₹44,900 to ₹1,42,400)

verified Eligibility Criteria

  • check_circle

    Educational Qualification

    Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized University or equivalent. (Final year students can also apply provided they get result before cutoff date).

  • check_circle

    Age Limit (As on 01-08-2024)

    Minimum 18 years and Maximum 32 years. (Age relaxation applicable as per govt rules for reserved categories).
