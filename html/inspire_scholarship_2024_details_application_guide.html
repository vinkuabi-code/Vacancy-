Innovation in Science Pursuit for Inspired Research (INSPIRE) Scholarship 2024

account_balance Department of Science & Technology (DST), Govt. of India

Reward Amount

₹80,000 / Year

Duration

Up to 5 Years

Scholarship Overview

The INSPIRE Scholarship for Higher Education (SHE) is a flagship scheme of the Department of Science and Technology (DST). It aims to attract meritorious youth to study basic and natural sciences at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels. By providing financial assistance, the government seeks to foster a culture of research and development, ensuring a steady stream of scientific talent for India's growing innovation ecosystem.

Eligibility Criteria

  • check_circle

    Age Limit

    Applicants must be between 17 and 22 years of age as of the registration date.

  • check_circle

    Academic Performance

    Must be among the top 1% meritorious students in their respective Class 12th Board Examinations (State or Central).

  • check_circle

    Current Enrollment

    Students must be enrolled in a regular Bachelor's (BSc) or Integrated Master's (Int. MSc) course in Basic or Natural Sciences.

Application Process

1

Online Registration

Visit the official INSPIRE-DST portal and create a user account with valid email and mobile number.

2

Filling Application

Complete the application form with personal, academic, and bank account details accurately.

3

Document Upload

Upload scanned copies of all required certificates in PDF format as per specified size limits.

4

Final Submission

Review all entries and submit. Take a printout of the generated application for future reference.
